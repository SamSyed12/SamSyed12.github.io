About Me

I've always been drawn to uncovering what's really happening beneath the surface. Whether it's spending hours reading investigative journalism or diving into a dataset, I find genuine excitement in discovering patterns and relationships that tell a deeper story. Currently pursuing my Master's in Data Science at UBC (following a dual degree in Computer Science and Neuroscience from Skidmore College in the United States), I've channeled this curiosity into building predictive models, designing interactive dashboards, and translating complex data into actionable insights. From customer segmentation analysis at Snow Data Science to developing machine learning classifiers, I'm driven by the challenge of getting closer to what's true—and making those truths accessible to others. When I'm not working with data, you'll find me watching movies, reading or on the road with friends. If you'd like to talk about data, potential opportunities, or just connect, feel free to reach out—I'm always happy to chat.
